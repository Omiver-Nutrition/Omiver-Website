1. Data Collection

At Omiver, we collect information to deliver a personalized and adaptive wellness platform. The categories of information we collect include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, mailing address, and billing information.
  • Demographic Data: Age, sex, and lifestyle indicators necessary to calibrate biological models.
  • Biomarker & Physiological Data: Physiological indicators and biomarker levels measured from samples processed by our independent laboratory partners.
  • Technical Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device properties, and navigation interactions collected via standard secure logs.

2. How We Use Data

We process your information under rigorous security constraints for the following purposes:

  • Generating customized wellness, exercise, and dietary recommendations.
  • Improving our predictive algorithms and machine-learning models to increase advice accuracy.
  • Processing payments and shipping testing kits to your address.
  • Communicating critical updates regarding your account, reports, or security alerts.

We do not use your sensitive biological biomarker metrics for general targeted advertising.

3. Third-Party Labs

To perform molecular analysis, we contract with accredited independent laboratory facilities. When you submit a sample:

  • We transfer the sample to the lab with a pseudonymized, randomized ID to protect your name and personal identifiers.
  • The lab processes the sample and transmits raw biomarker values back to Omiver.
  • The lab is strictly prohibited from storing, sharing, or using your biological sample or data for any other commercial purpose.

4. Security Standards

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST BY DESIGN

We treat your biological data as highly sensitive information. Omiver strictly separates your personal identification keys (like billing name and email) from your biological biomarker data. Our system utilizes end-to-end TLS encryption in transit and high-grade AES-256 encryption for database records at rest.

We limit internal database access to authorized staff members under strict multi-factor authentication protocols and logging requirements. While we make every effort to safeguard data.

5. Cookies & Web Tech

We use cookies and secure local storage tokens to improve user experience and keep you logged in during your session. These consist of:

  • Essential Cookies: Crucial for navigating the platform and completing session checkouts securely.
  • Performance Cookies: Helping us understand how visitors interact with pages to fix performance bugs and layout issues.

You can manage cookie preferences directly in your web browser settings, though blocking essential cookies may disable parts of our website.

6. Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide your personalized wellness reports or comply with regulatory standards.

If you choose to delete your account, we will permanently purge or fully anonymize all biological biomarker profiles and personal identifiers from our production databases within 30 days of your request.

7. User Legal Rights

Depending on your location (such as California or the European Economic Area), you may have legal rights regarding your data, including:

  • Right to Access: The right to request a comprehensive copy of all biological reports and metrics we store.
  • Right to Portability: The right to download your raw biomarker records in a standard digital format.
  • Right to Erasure: The right to request complete deletion of your profile from our databases.
  • Right to Rectification: The right to request corrections to inaccurate demographic details.

To exercise these rights, please submit a request to info@omiver.me.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to adapt to changes in technologies, legal frameworks, or clinical laboratory processes. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date timestamp at the top.

If you continue to use Omiver Services after changes take effect, you agree to the updated terms and policies.
