This Privacy Notice for Omiver, LLC describes how we access, collect, store, use, and share your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, and related Services.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Services.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect information you provide directly, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, usernames, passwords, billing details, contact preferences, and authentication data. We may also collect sensitive information such as health data, biometric data, genetic data, financial data, and racial or ethnic origin where permitted by law or with consent.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process information to create and manage accounts, authenticate users, provide Services, support inquiries, fulfill orders, process payments, communicate with you, protect our Services, improve our products, and comply with legal obligations.

3. WHAT LEGAL BASES DO WE RELY ON?

We process your information when we have a valid legal reason, such as consent, contract performance, legitimate business interests, or compliance with law, depending on your jurisdiction.

4. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We may share information with service providers, affiliates, business partners, laboratories, payment processors, and other third parties only as needed to provide the Services or complete a transaction.

5. DO WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain security, remember preferences, prevent crashes, fix bugs, analyze usage, and support basic site functions.

6. DO WE OFFER ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-BASED PRODUCTS?

We offer AI-powered features to improve the experience and provide personalized wellness guidance. Personal information processed by these tools is handled in line with this Privacy Policy.

7. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it when possible.

8. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

We use reasonable organizational and technical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age, or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction.

10. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict certain personal information, and to withdraw consent where applicable. Contact info@omiver.me to make a request.

11. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

At this time, we do not respond to DNT browser signals because no uniform industry standard has been finalized.

12. DO UNITED STATES RESIDENTS HAVE SPECIFIC PRIVACY RIGHTS?

If you are a resident of certain U.S. states, you may have rights to request access to, correct, delete, or receive details about personal information we maintain, and to opt out of targeted advertising, selling, or profiling where applicable.

13. DO WE MAKE UPDATES TO THIS NOTICE?

Yes. We may update this notice from time to time to reflect changes to our Services or to remain compliant with laws. The updated version will be indicated by a revised date at the top of this page.

14. HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US ABOUT THIS NOTICE?

If you have questions or comments about this notice, contact us at info@omiver.me.

15. HOW CAN YOU REVIEW, UPDATE, OR DELETE YOUR DATA?

You may request access to the personal information we collect from you, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or request deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email above.
